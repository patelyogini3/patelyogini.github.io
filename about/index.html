About

Hello there, I am Yogini Patel and I’m a Computer Engineer Student majoring in Nanoscale System Design at the University of Alberta.

I have always been passionate about computers, software programming and technology as a whole. The thrill of building something I envisioned in my mind and watching it work is something I’ve always been a fan of. Recently I have been interested in Machine Learning and have been taking out time daily to broaden my knowledge about machine learning and data science.

I love discussions with people who are driven to solve important problems. I’d love to talk with you about your ideas and I can be reached via email.

Areas of Interest: Robotics, Embedded systems, Knowledge-Based AI, Machine Learning, Nanoscale System Design
